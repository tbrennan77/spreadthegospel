Getting the Gospel to the Philippines

Our purpose is to reach the poor people of the Philippines with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Learn More

My name is Froi Medina born in Baguio City, Philippines. At the age of 11 years old my family and I moved to Los Angeles California. I got saved when I was 15 years old and started preaching at 15 years old: I was the first person who got saved in our entire family. Now my parents and sisters are now saved and currently serving in the ministry. Some of my relatives are are currently active in in a Baptist church. At the age of 17 years old God called me to be a missionary in Baguio City. I was also a youth pastor of our church at 17 years old. I went to Bible school in my young adult years and graduated in 2003. In March 19,2006 we had our first service in Baguio City, Philippines with an attendance of 350 and 32 baptized.

We reach Baguio City and surrounding areas. We have helped plant the Lighthouse Bible Baptist Church of Urayon Caba, La Union and Lighthouse Bible Baptist Church of Maliwalo Tarlac City.

We are praying to start an orphanage—we currently have 3 orphans in our church—and a Christian school—mainly for those who cannot afford to go to school.

We’ve distributed over 500,000 Bibles since 2010
More about our Bible Distribution Ministry

Ministries

Our focus is not only to help feed the hungry, shelter the homeless, and provide medical help to the needy, but primarily in sharing the Gospel of our Saviour. Lives are being changed here in the Philippines, and we hope you will share with us this amazing opportunity.


Soul Winning

Since 2006 our Ministry has seen over 20,000 people Accept Christ as Saviour and over 6,000 people followed the Lord in Baptism.

Bible Distribution

Bibles are very expensive in our country more expensive than in the US. As people from the US send us Bibles we give the people Bibles. We have past out over 500,000 Bibles since 2010.

Feeding

To feed the miss fortune kids.

Kids

The LOCK—Lighthouse Outreach Community Kids—ministry is design to reach miss fortunate children in our community. We teach them Bible doctrines, feed them and we play basketball.

Politicians

We are also striving to reach the politicians in our area.

Basketball

Since the Filipino people favorite sports is basketball we started this ministry to reach young men and men. This is the most effective ministry we have to get men to our church.
